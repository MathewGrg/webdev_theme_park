The Solar System Expedition

There is no shortage of admirers and thrill seekers for our most popular attraction, the Solar System Expedition, due to its roller coaster resembling ride, which takes the riders through a close to realitic journey from one end to the other end of the the Solar System, with fun facts about the whole Solar System and its planets whilst passing by each, planet like structures.
