General Admission Ticket

Information

This ticket allows you to access all three available attractions, including the Swing Ride, the Roller Coaster and the Ferris Wheel (the number of rides in not limited).

It is valid for one day and the entire park will be open to you, there are no restricted areas.

Access for the current special attraction "The Solar System Expedition" can be directly booked at the Space Park for an additional fee of $5.00 per person (children only pay $3.00).

Multi-Day Ticket

Information

This ticket allows you to access all three available attractions, including the Swing Ride, the Roller Coaster and the Ferris Wheel (the number of rides in not limited).

A Multi-Day Ticket is valid for three consecutive days and the entire park will be open to you, there are no restricted areas.

Access for the current special attraction "The Solar System Expedition" can be directly booked at the Space Park for an additional fee of $5.00 per person and per ride (children only pay $3.00 per ride).

Pricing

Children Students Adults
$40.00 $60.00 $80.00

Member Ticket

Information

This ticket allows you to access all three available attractions, including the Swing Ride, the Roller Coaster and the Ferris Wheel (the number of rides in not limited).

A Member Ticket is valid for one entire year and the entire park will be open to you, there are no restricted areas.

Access for the current special attraction "The Solar System Expedition" is included within the Member Ticket.

Pricing

Children Students Adults
$300.00 $500.00 $700.00
